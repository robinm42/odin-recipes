AJ's Jambalaya

Description

Jambalaya is what happens when a whole lot of scrumptious Creole flavors get cooked up into one pleasing dish.

Ingredients

  • Andouille sausage, sliced, 2 links
  • Chicken, cubed, 1lb
  • Shrimp, cooked, 1/2 lb (optional)
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Butter, 1/4 cup
  • Onion, diced, 1 cup
  • Celery, diced, 1 cup
  • Green bell pepper, diced, 1 cup
  • Vegetable broth, 2 (14 oz) cans
  • Diced Tomatoes, 1 (14.5 oz) can
  • Tomato sauce, 1 (14 oz) can
  • Uncooked Rice, 1 1/2 cups
  • Water, 1 cup
  • The Spice Hunter Cajun Creole Seasoning, 2+ tsp

Steps

  1. Cook sliced sausage in a large skillet over medium­high heat until cooked through, remove from pan and set aside
  2. Pour oil into pan, add chicken and cook thoroughly, then remove from pan and set aside with the sausage
  3. Add butter and vegetables to pan and cook over medium heat until softened, but not browned
  4. Next add broth, tomatoes, tomato sauce, rice, water and seasoning, stirring to combine
  5. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer 20­25 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed
  6. If liquid is absorbed, but rice is not yet cooked, add an additional 1⁄2 cup water as needed
  7. Stir sausage and chicken into pan, top with shrimp, if desired, and cook for an additional 5 minutes
  8. Serve and relish!
