Crispy Oven Baked Chicken Wings

Description

Use the magic of baking powder and a wire rack to make baked chicken wings come out with a satisfying crispy crunchy skin.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings, cut into wingettes and drummettes, 3 lbs
  • Baking powder, 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder, 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder, 1 tsp
  • Onion powder, 1 tsp
  • Chili powder, 1 tsp
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Wash chicken and pat dry (Do not add any oil)
  3. Combine the baking powder and powdered seasonings in a bowl and mix together well
  4. Toss the wingettes and drumettes with the dry coating
  5. Spray the wire rack with oil to prevent sticking
  6. Arrange the chicken pieces on a wire rack over a baking pan and place in the preheated oven
  7. Cook wings for 50-60 minutes, turning every 20 minutes until crispy and done
  8. Eat them salt & pepper style, or with your favorite sauce!
